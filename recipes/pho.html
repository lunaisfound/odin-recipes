Authentic Pho Recipe

Pho is a traditional Vietnamese dish from the North of Vietnam. Its history is unknown but many believe the dish dates back to the days of the American War.

Ingredients

  • Vermicelli Noodles
  • Beef Flavour cube
  • Chicken Breast

Steps

  1. Step 1. Boil the chicken bones
  2. Step 2. Once the water's boiled, add in charred onions and gingers
  3. Step 3. Put in your choice of spices
  4. Step 4. Cook the noodles as instructed by the packaging
  5. Step 5. Shred the cooked chicken breast and add them into your bowl of Pho
  6. Step 6. Enjoy!
